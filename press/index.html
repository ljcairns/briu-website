Company overview

Briu is an AI consultancy that deploys production-ready agents and workflows for businesses. We handle the build so companies get in-house-caliber AI operations without hiring a dedicated team. Our agents run on transparent infrastructure at a fraction of traditional costs, typically $2-5 per day.

The name Briu comes from Catalan, meaning energy, push, and courage. It reflects how we work: directly, efficiently, and without the overhead that makes most AI projects stall.

Founder

Briu was founded by Lucas Cairns. Lucas builds AI agents and workflows for businesses, combining hands-on engineering with strategic consulting. He works directly with clients from scoping through deployment, keeping teams small and quality high.

What we do

Briu deploys AI agents that handle real business operations: drafting and sending emails, updating CRMs, generating reports, and orchestrating multi-step workflows across tools a company already uses. Every agent runs on the client's own infrastructure with full transparency into costs and behavior.

Services include

  • Kickoff engagement — a focused sprint to identify and deploy the first production agent
  • Workshop add-on — team-wide AI briefing tailored to the company's stack and goals
  • Implementation — single or multi-workflow builds for deeper automation
  • Retainer — ongoing support, monitoring, and iteration
  • Speaking — keynotes and half-day workshops on practical AI deployment
