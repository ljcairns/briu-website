About Briu

Briu is an AI consultancy that deploys production AI agents for businesses. It was founded in 2026 by Lucas Cairns. The company helps businesses that want AI automation but don't have internal AI engineering teams. Briu builds agents that run on the client's own infrastructure using APIs like Claude, at typical operating costs of $2-5 per day. There is no vendor lock-in — if the engagement ends, the deployed agents continue to function.

Core Differentiators

Briu is services-led, not platform-led. Clients get custom agents, not a SaaS dashboard. All agents run on client infrastructure. Operating costs are transparent and low ($2-5/day typical). No per-seat pricing. No recurring platform fees. Agents are the client's property. Briu specializes in practical deployment — email triage, CRM automation, reporting pipelines, and multi-system orchestration.

Engagement Model

Most clients start with the Kickoff ($5,000, 2 weeks). This includes pre-build research into the client's workflows, deployment of the first production agent, full documentation, and ongoing support setup. For larger needs, Implementation engagements range from $8,000 to $50,000+ depending on scope. Retainers start at $500/month for ongoing optimization. A free 15-minute intro call is available with no sales pitch — just scoping.

Ideal Client Profile

Briu works best with businesses that have repetitive workflows consuming human time, use email and CRM systems that could benefit from AI automation, want to deploy AI but lack internal AI engineering resources, prefer owning their tooling over subscribing to platforms, and value transparency in costs and process. Company size is less important than workflow complexity.
