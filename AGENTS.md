# Repository Guidance

## Project Shape
- This repo is a small static marketing site centered on `index.html`.
- Prefer minimal, incremental edits over broad rewrites.
- Preserve the existing visual language unless a change is explicitly approved.

## Brand Positioning
- Briu is a premium, founder-led AI implementation company.
- Do not frame Briu as a generic consultant, agency, or "AI automation shop."
- Do not frame Briu as services-only; it is services-led now and productizing over time.
- Briu combines services, systems, and an emerging product/SaaS layer.
- Current work includes consulting, architecture, implementation, and retainers.
- Long-term direction includes productized offerings, reusable software, and SaaS.

## Messaging Rules
- Preserve founder voice and substantive point of view.
- Optimize homepage copy for clarity, trust, and conversion.
- Move long market-thesis or manifesto-style material into Insights pages.
- Reduce hype, absolutist claims, and generic AI language.
- Emphasize practical deployment, control, governance, cost clarity, and execution.
- The brand should feel premium, thoughtful, and operationally credible.

## Proof And Content
- Build openly where useful; Briu's own systems and site iterations can serve as proof.
- Preserve strong ideas already in the site, but place them in the right information architecture.
- Keep fear/uncertainty around AI agents in view and address it with clarity and trust.

## Working Style
- Before changing `index.html`, prefer documenting structure and proposing the smallest safe edit.
- When adding pages, keep them static and aligned with the current architecture unless approved otherwise.
