Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

Briu is a small AI consultancy. We keep things simple: we collect only what we need to respond to your inquiry, we do not track you across the web, and we do not sell your data.

What we collect

When you submit our contact form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your company name (optional)
  • Your responses to our intake questions (how you use AI today, what you'd like to automate)

We do not collect any data from you automatically. There are no cookies, no analytics scripts, no tracking pixels, and no fingerprinting on this website.

How we use your data

We use the information you submit solely to respond to your inquiry with relevant ideas for your business. We may follow up by email if you've reached out to us. That's it.

Third parties

The following third-party services are involved in operating this website:

  • FormSubmit.co — processes our contact form submissions and forwards them to our email. Their privacy policy is available at formsubmit.co/privacy.pdf.
  • GitHub Pages — hosts this website. GitHub may collect standard server logs (IP addresses, browser type). See GitHub's privacy statement.
  • Cloudflare — provides CDN and DNS services. See Cloudflare's privacy policy.
  • Google Fonts — serves the typefaces used on this site. See Google's privacy policy.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with anyone else.

Cookies and tracking

This website does not use cookies. We do not run any analytics or tracking software. We have no way to identify you unless you choose to contact us.

Data retention

We retain contact form submissions in our email for as long as needed to respond to and manage the business relationship. If you'd like us to delete your information, email us and we will do so promptly.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy or your data, reach us at hi@briu.ai.
